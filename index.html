The Problem

Global platforms
were not built
for Pakistan

Fiverr, Upwork, and Freelancer.com operate under financial structures that systematically disadvantage Pakistani professionals. The cost is real, measurable, and ongoing.

01
Fee structures of 10 to 20 percent

Tiered commission models take a compounding cut of every transaction. A freelancer earning $30,000 annually loses between $3,000 and $6,000 to platform fees before a single expense is paid.

02
Mandatory 14-day payment cooldowns

Funds earned cannot be withdrawn for two weeks after a project closes. For professionals managing monthly obligations, this restriction creates predictable, recurring financial stress.

03
No domestic payment support

None of the major platforms offer native integration with Pakistani mobile wallets or local bank transfers. Every withdrawal passes through costly international intermediaries.

04
Informal work carries no protection

Social media freelancing — the default alternative — offers no escrow, no structured contract, and no recourse when a client refuses to pay or a freelancer fails to deliver.

LOCALIZED. SECURE.
AI-ASSISTED.

PakFreelance replaces every one of these gaps with purpose-built infrastructure — domestic payments, enforced escrow, intelligent planning tools, and verified identities on both sides of every contract.

Platform Features

Everything the
lifecycle needs

Ten modules cover the complete arc from user onboarding to final payment settlement — without gaps.

Milestone-Based Escrow System

Client funds are deposited per milestone and held until verified delivery. No money changes hands without both parties confirming completion. The admin layer provides a final audit trail on every transaction, with webhook callbacks from Stripe and Zindagi confirming settlement.

KYC Verification

CNIC-based identity checks via Sumsub ensure every user is a real, traceable individual. AI flags inconsistencies before admin review.

Local Payment Integration

Zindagi wallet and direct bank transfer. No currency conversion delays. No foreign intermediary cuts.

Project Bidding Marketplace

Clients post structured briefs. Freelancers submit AI-assisted proposals with optimised bid amounts and persuasive pitch generation.

Gig Marketplace

Freelancers list tiered service packages with AI-generated copy, SEO visibility scores, and real-time performance analytics.

Dispute Resolution

AI synthesises evidence, chat logs, and milestone history into settlement recommendations. Admins issue binding decisions with one click.

Multi-Freelancer Teams

Team-based contracts with AI-recommended role assignments, shared milestone tracking, and proportional payment distribution.

Secure Real-Time Messaging

WebSocket chat with AI summaries, voice note transcription, file versioning, and group channels for team projects.

Admin Analytics Dashboard

Centralised oversight of escrow balances, fraud alerts, dispute queues, and platform commission — exportable to PDF or CSV.

Platform Governance & Moderation

Comprehensive system administration tools that give full control over user activities, financial audits, platform disputes, and policy enforcement.
